## INGREDIENTS

### Cake

- 2 large eggs
- 200g sugar
- 150g neutral oil (or butter)
- 150g flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp bicarbonate or salt
- 1 tbsp cinnamon
- 2 carrots (about 200g grated)
- 50g walnuts (optional, or almonds / hazelnuts)
- 50g grated coconut
- 2 oranges for zesting
- 1cm of ginger

### Frosting

- 125g fresh cream
- 100g cream cheese (philadelphia)
- 50g sugar

## DIRECTIONS

1. Pre-heat the oven at 180°C
2. Mix the eggs with the sugar until they turn white
3. Add the oil and mix
4. Add the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate or salt, cinnamon, mix well
5. Grate the carrots
6. Add the grated carrots, coconut, orange zest and grated ginger, mix very well
7. Lay a baking sheet in your tray, add the preparation to the tray
8. Bake in the oven for 1h - 1h20 at 130°C for a moist cake you can keep several days
9. Optional: Mix the fresh cream and the cream cheese well at room temperature, add the sugar and keep refrigerated until the cake cools down
10. Add frosting on top of the cake
