## INGREDIENTS

- 2 bananas (160g peeled)
- 20g grape seed oil (or olive oil)
- 60g of sugar
- 10g muscovado (or sugar)
- 100g flour
- 4g baking powder
- 40g butter (melted)
- 1 egg (50g)
- 1 lime (peel and juice)

## DIRECTIONS

1. Cook the whole bananas for 20 min at 200°C.
2. Peel and mash with a fork, add oil.
3. Mix the sugars with the zest then add the egg. Whisk well.
4. Mix the flour with the baking powder and add to the first mixture. Add the melted butter. Add the banana compote.
5. Pour the dough into a buttered and lightly floured mold.
6. Place banana slices on top. Sprinkle with sugar.
7. Bake for 40 minutes at 150°C.
8. Make a syrup with lemon juice, rum and sugar.
9. Brush the cake at the exit of the oven.
