## INGREDIENTS

### Base

- 1 can of pineapple
- 150g of flour
- 150g of sugar + 100g of sugar for caramel
- 150g of butter (melted)
- 3 eggs
- 4g baking powder
- A pinch of salt

### Optional

- lime (zest)
- cinnamon powder
- nutmeg powder
- vanilla
- rum

## DIRECTIONS

1. Heat the oven to 180°C
2. Prepare a dry caramel by putting 100g of sugar in a pan and melt it
3. Pour the caramel in a circular cake pan, spread evenly and place the pineapple slices in the pan
4. Melt the butter on low heat
5. Beat the 150g of sugar with the eggs until the mass doubles
6. Add the melted butter, mix well
7. Mix the flour, baking powder and salt together
8. Add the powders progressively to the batter, mix well
9. Add pineapple pieces to the batter, mix well
10. Add your flavoring of choice (rum, zest, cinnamon, nutmeg), mix well
11. Pour the batter in the baking pan
12. Bake for 35-40 min
13. Take out of the oven, use the can syrup to wet the cake and serve on a plate
