## INGREDIENTS

- 250g dark chocolate
- 250g butter
- 250g sugar
- 70g flour
- 4 eggs

## DIRECTIONS

1. Start the oven at 200°C
2. Melt the chocolate in a bain-marie, add the soft butter and aim for a smooth texture
3. Add the sugar and mix
4. Add the flour and mix
5. Break the eggs and beat them, add them to base and mix well
6. Lightly grease and flour a baking pan (round 24 cm)
7. Lower the temperature to 150°C
8. Bake for 30 min
9. Let the cake cool down for 10 minutes and remove it from the pan

## NOTES

It's possible to adjust the quantities (less butter and sugar).
