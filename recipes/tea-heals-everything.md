## INGREDIENTS

- 10 cardamom pods
- 10 cloves seeds
- 10 saffron threads
- Ginger
- Cinnamon
- Turmeric
- Pepper
- Lemon
- Honey

## DIRECTIONS

1. Mash a thumb of ginger, 10 cardamom seeds, 10 clove seeds, 10 saffron threads, a pinch of cinnamon, another of turmeric and another of pepper

2. Bring this mixture to a boil with water (measured in 2 cups) and let it boil for 2-4 minutes

3. Turn off and add the juice of half a lemon and approximately 3 tsp of honey and mix well

4. Let it rest for about 5 minutes, strain and drink
