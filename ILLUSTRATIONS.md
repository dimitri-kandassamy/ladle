# Illustration prompts

> Generated by `tools/illustration_prompts.py` — re-run it after adding recipes.

Each recipe needs one hero illustration. Generate it with any image model (these prompts are tuned for Google's **nano banana** / Gemini 2.5 Flash Image, but work anywhere), then **save it as the PNG path shown** — the build automatically prefers that PNG over the bundled SVG placeholder, with no front-matter changes.

All prompts share one locked watercolour-and-ink style so the set stays coherent. Generate on a **transparent background** and export a **PNG** (or WebP) so the page's cream paper and grain show through cleanly — the soft cast shadow keeps the dish grounded on the page. (If your tool can't make a true transparent background, generate on a plain flat colour and remove it, watching for edge fringing.)

**Recommended size for every recipe image:** 3:2 landscape — generate at the largest size your tool offers (nano banana / Gemini ≈ 1024 px on the long edge; upscale toward ~1500 px wide for crisp print), and export as a PNG with a transparent background.

## Decorative pages (optional)

The cover, endpapers and back use generated white **line-art** patterns on navy (`assets/illustrations/patterns/*.svg`). To replace them with AI art, generate **2:3 portrait, full-bleed (≈1024×1536)** images, save as `patterns/cover.png` / `endpaper.png` / `back.png`, and point the matching `url(...)` in `assets/css/print.css` at the new file.

```text
A seamless full-bleed pattern of simple single continuous-line white ink drawings of food (vegetables, fruit, jars, utensils, plates, bottles) evenly scattered on a deep navy background (#16203a), minimal line-art style, consistent line weight, no fills, no text.
```
