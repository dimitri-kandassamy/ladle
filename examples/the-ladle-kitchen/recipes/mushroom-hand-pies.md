## INGREDIENTS

### Filling

- 300g mushrooms, finely chopped
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 shallot, minced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh thyme
- 2 tbsp cream cheese
- Salt and pepper

### Pastry

- 1 sheet shortcrust pastry (about 320g)
- 1 egg, beaten

## DIRECTIONS

1. Melt the butter and cook the shallot and garlic until soft.
2. Add the mushrooms and thyme; cook until the liquid evaporates.
3. Stir in the cream cheese, season, and let the filling cool.
4. Cut the pastry into six rounds. Spoon filling onto each, fold, and crimp.
5. Brush with the beaten egg and cut a small vent in the top.
6. Bake at 200°C for 20-25 minutes, until golden.

## NOTES

- These freeze well before baking — glaze and bake straight from frozen.
