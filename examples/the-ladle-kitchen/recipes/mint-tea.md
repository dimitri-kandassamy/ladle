## INGREDIENTS

- 1 large handful of fresh mint leaves
- 2 tsp green tea
- 500ml water
- Honey, to taste

## DIRECTIONS

1. Bring the water almost to a boil, then let it settle for a minute.
2. Add the green tea and steep for 2 minutes.
3. Add the mint and steep for another 2 minutes.
4. Strain into cups and sweeten with honey to taste.

## NOTES

- Use plenty of mint — it should be the loudest thing in the cup.
