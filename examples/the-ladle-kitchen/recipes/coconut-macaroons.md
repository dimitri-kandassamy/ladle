## INGREDIENTS

- 200g desiccated coconut
- 150g sugar
- 2 egg whites
- 1 pinch of salt

## DIRECTIONS

1. Heat the oven to 160°C and line a tray.
2. Whisk the egg whites with the salt to soft peaks.
3. Fold in the sugar, then the coconut, until the mixture holds together.
4. Shape into small mounds and place on the tray.
5. Bake for 15-18 minutes, until the tips are golden.

## NOTES

- Dip the cooled bases in melted dark chocolate for a fancier version.
