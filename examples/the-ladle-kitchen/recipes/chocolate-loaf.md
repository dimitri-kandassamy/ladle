## INGREDIENTS

### Loaf

- 180g flour
- 40g cocoa powder
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 150g sugar
- 3 eggs
- 120ml neutral oil
- 120ml milk

### Glaze

- 60g dark chocolate
- 20g butter

## DIRECTIONS

1. Heat the oven to 170°C and line a loaf tin.
2. Whisk the flour, cocoa, baking powder, and sugar in a bowl.
3. Beat in the eggs, oil, and milk until smooth.
4. Pour into the tin and bake for 45 minutes, until a skewer comes out clean.
5. Melt the chocolate and butter together, then pour over the cooled loaf.
