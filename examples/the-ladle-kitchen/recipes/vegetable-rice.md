## INGREDIENTS

- 200g long-grain rice
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 carrots, diced
- 1 red pepper, diced
- 100g peas (fresh or frozen)
- 400ml vegetable stock
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt and pepper

## DIRECTIONS

1. Warm the oil in a wide pan over medium heat. Soften the onion for 5 minutes.
2. Add the carrots and pepper and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring.
3. Stir in the rice and paprika until the grains glisten.
4. Pour in the stock, bring to a simmer, then cover and cook for 15 minutes.
5. Fold in the peas, cover, and rest off the heat for 5 minutes.
6. Fluff with a fork, season, and serve.

## NOTES

- Any firm vegetable works — swap in courgette, corn, or green beans.
- Leftovers reheat well with a splash of water.
