## INGREDIENTS

- 200g dark chocolate
- 120g butter
- 3 eggs
- 100g sugar
- 50g flour

## DIRECTIONS

1. Start the oven at 160°C
2. Grease and flour a baking pan (Use a round preferably (22cm))
3. Melt the chocolate with the butter in a bain-marie (or microwave)
4. Beat the eggs with the sugar, add the sifted flour
5. Add the melted chocolate and butter, mix gently
6. Pour into the pan and put in the oven for 12-13 minutes
7. Let it cool down and enjoy
