## INGREDIENTS

- 500g puff pastry
- 250g veal
- 250g pork loin or neck
- 1 large shallot
- 10cl of white wine
- parsley
- salt
- pepper
- 1 egg for glazing

## DIRECTIONS

1. Cut the meat into strips
2. Chop the shallots and parsley
3. Mix everything with salt, pepper and white wine
4. Leave to marinate for 12h in the fridge
5. Put the meat in a sieve and try to lose as much juice as possible
6. Cut the puff pastry in large rectangles
7. Place the meat in the center of the rectangle (squeeze well to remove the juice)
8. Fold the corners, cover with another rectangle
9. Make 3 holes on the surface, brush with egg wash and add decorations
10. Bake 15 min at 200°C then 35 min at 170°C
