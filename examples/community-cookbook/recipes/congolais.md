## INGREDIENTS

- 300g sugar
- 200g grated coconut
- 5 egg whites
- 2 tbsp flour
- vanilla extract

## DIRECTIONS

1. Start the oven at 180ºC
2. Beat the egg whites until foamy
3. Mix the sugar, coconut and flour together
4. Add the vanilla extract and any additional flavoring
5. Add the beaten egg whites gently
6. Line up cookies on a tray
7. Bake in the oven for 10-13 min and let them cool down
