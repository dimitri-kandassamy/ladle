## INGREDIENTS

- 300g white rice (carolino or agulha)
- 750 ml water
- 1 onion
- 2 large carrots
- 3 tbsp of olive oil
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt

## DIRECTIONS

1. Dice the onion and grate the carrots
2. In a large sauce pan, add the olive oil then the onion and the bay leaf cut in half
3. When the onions are translucent, add the carrots, mix well until the carrots are soft
4. Add the rice and mix until it gets translucent
5. Seasoned with salt, add the water, mix and cook covered for 14 minutes
6. Serve immediately
