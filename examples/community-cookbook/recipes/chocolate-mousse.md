## INGREDIENTS

- 125g cream (fat)
- 58g milk (reduced fat)
- 27g brown sugar
- 170g dark chocolate (65%)
- 40g milk chocolate (35%)
- 175g egg white

## DIRECTIONS

1. Warm up the milk and cream to 80°C
2. Pour the milk on the chocolates and mix while keeping the whisk at the center
3. Whip the egg whites with the sugar
4. Add the egg whites once the chocolate temperature reaches 29°C (Use the whisk with 1/3 first)
5. Keep in the fridge for 4 hours
