## INGREDIENTS

### Base

- 175g graham crackers
- 3 tbsp of sugar
- 70g butter
- 500g cream cheese
- 400g sweetened condensed milk
- 80ml lime juice + zest (2 tsp)
- 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
- 120ml heavy cream
- A pinch of salt (1/2 tsp)
- 1 lime for decoration

## DIRECTIONS

1. Whisk together the crumbled cracker, sugar and salt. Stir in the chilled melted butter until the mixture looks like wet sand and holds together.
2. Press the crust into a springform pan and freeze until firm for 15 min
3. Beat cream cheese and condensed milk until fluffy (5 min or more)
4. Add lime juice, zest and vanilla extract (1 min)
5. Beat the heavy cream into whipped cream and gently fold in into the mixture
6. Pour over the crust and refrigerate for at least 12h and up to 3 days
7. Unmold and serve garnished with lime rounds
