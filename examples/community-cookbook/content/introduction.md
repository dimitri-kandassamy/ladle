This is a cookbook built by a community, one pull request at a time. Every recipe
here started as a plain markdown file written by someone who wanted to share a dish
they love — a weeknight staple, a family standard, or a small experiment that
turned out well enough to pass on.

We collect those files, and a build pipeline turns them into the book you are
reading now: a designed PDF for printing and a reflowable EPUB for your reader.
Nothing here is locked away in a proprietary format. The source is markdown, the
fonts are open, the artwork is our own, and anyone can add the next recipe.

Cooking is one of the oldest ways we take care of each other. We hope these recipes
find their way into your kitchen, get splattered and annotated and adapted, and
remind you that good food — like good software — is better when it is shared.

Add yours: open the repository, copy the recipe template, and send it our way.
