## INGREDIENTS

- 200g plain flour
- 200g sugar
- 3 eggs
- 120ml olive oil
- 120ml milk
- 2 tsp baking powder
- Zest of 1 lemon and a pinch of salt

## DIRECTIONS

1. Heat the oven to 180°C and line an 8-inch tin.
2. Whisk the eggs and sugar until pale, then stream in the oil and milk.
3. Fold in the flour, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest until just combined.
4. Pour into the tin and bake 35–40 minutes, until a skewer comes out clean.

## NOTES

- Keeps beautifully for two days, wrapped — the crumb only gets more tender.
