## INGREDIENTS

- 60g fresh ginger, grated
- 3 tbsp honey or sugar
- Juice of 2 limes
- 750ml sparkling water
- Ice, and mint to finish

## DIRECTIONS

1. Steep the grated ginger in 100ml hot water for 10 minutes, then strain.
2. Stir the honey and lime juice into the warm ginger water until dissolved.
3. Divide over ice and top with sparkling water.
4. Finish each glass with a sprig of mint.

## NOTES

- Make the ginger-lime base ahead; top with bubbles only when you serve.
