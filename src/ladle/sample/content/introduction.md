This little book exists to show off a theme. It carries one recipe in each
section — something savory, something sweet, something to drink — so a preview
can exercise the cover, the contents page, a recipe opener, and a method page all
at once.

The words here are filler; the point is the type, the colour, and the layout the
theme brings to them. Swap in a different theme and every page redraws itself
around the same plain markdown.
